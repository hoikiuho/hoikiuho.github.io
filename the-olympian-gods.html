The Twelve Olympian Gods

Are listed below for your reference, in the order they were born. (Hades was not counted as an Olympian god.)

After Zeus, it won't definitive about who was born first, because they are the second generation, but I'll try my best.

  1. Aphrodite (risen from Ouranos' ichor and sea foam)
  2. Hestia (some say Hestia gave her throne to Dionysus, depending on the myth)
  3. Demeter
  4. Hera
  5. Poseidon
  6. Zeus
  7. Athena
  8. Ares
  9. Apollo
  10. Artemis
  11. Hephaestus
  12. Hermes
